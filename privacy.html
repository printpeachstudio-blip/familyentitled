Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

The short version: We collect almost nothing. The information you enter into the entitlements tool is processed entirely in your browser and is never sent to our servers, stored in a database, or shared with anyone. We may use basic analytics to understand how people use the site. That's it.

1. Who we are

FamilyEntitled is an independent website providing a free entitlements calculation tool for UK families. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by HM Government or any government department.

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: hello@familyentitled.co.uk

2. What information we collect

Information you enter into the tool

The entitlements calculator asks you questions about your family situation — children's dates of birth, income bands, working status, and benefit status. This information is:

  • Processed entirely within your web browser using JavaScript
  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Never stored in any database
  • Never shared with any third party
  • Permanently erased when you close or refresh the page

We have designed the tool this way deliberately. We do not want your personal financial information, and we have no mechanism to receive or store it even if we did.

Analytics data

We may use a privacy-respecting analytics service (such as Plausible Analytics or a self-hosted alternative) to understand broad usage patterns — for example, how many people use the tool, which pages are most visited, and what country visitors are from. This data is:

  • Aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify individual visitors
  • Not linked to any information you enter into the tool
  • Does not use cookies or cross-site tracking

Cookies

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party cookies. If we use analytics, it is configured to work without cookies. We may use a single session cookie for basic website functionality (such as remembering your progress through the tool within a session), which is erased when you close your browser.

Display advertising

The site may display advertisements served by third-party ad networks (such as Google AdSense). These networks may use cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history. This is separate from anything you enter into our tool, which those networks cannot access. You can opt out of personalised advertising through Google's Ad Settings or by using browser settings to block third-party cookies.

3. What we do not collect

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your exact income or salary
  • Your children's names or exact dates of birth (these are used only within your browser for age calculations and immediately discarded)
  • Your National Insurance number
  • Any payment or financial account information

4. Your rights under UK GDPR

Because we do not collect or store personal data from the tool, there is nothing for us to share, correct, or delete on request. However, if you believe we hold any personal data about you (for example, from an email contact), you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we hold
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

5. Third-party links

Our tool provides links to GOV.UK and other official government websites. Once you leave our site, this privacy policy no longer applies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any sites you visit.

6. Children's privacy

Our tool is intended for use by adults (parents and guardians). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The dates of birth you enter for your children are processed only within your browser and are never transmitted or stored.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
